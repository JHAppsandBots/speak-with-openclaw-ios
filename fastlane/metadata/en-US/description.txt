Speak with Claw turns your iPhone into a voice interface for your AI bot. Just talk. No button, no hotword. The app detects when you stop speaking, sends your voice to your own server, and plays the response back.

Note: Responses are not instant — expect a few seconds per reply depending on your LLM and server setup.

Features:
- Hands-free conversation mode (VAD)
- Optional hotword activation
- Push-to-talk
- Audio responses play automatically
- Chat history per bot
- Multiple bots selectable
- Background mode
- On-device speech recognition, no cloud upload

Privacy:
This app sends your voice only to a server you configure and operate yourself. No data is sent to the developer or any third party. No analytics, no tracking, no backend.

Requirements:
- iPhone iOS 17+
- Mac running OpenClaw with voice relay server
- Local network or Tailscale

Setup: https://github.com/JHAppsandBots/speak-with-claw-ios
